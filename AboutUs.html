About Us
Why SMASH Badminton Club?
We have: 3 courts twice a week, friendly competitions, optional coaching (extra charge) and optional sport-specific training (free), players who are intermediate (SBA grades B, C and D), a social court for beginners/friends/partners on Saturdays You will get a good game – we grade players then use a software system to make sure you play different people your standard or above We estimate you will get five 20-minute games on Saturdays, and seven 20-minute games on Thursdays.
A lot of badminton for your money The club is run by a eight-person committee not-for-profit, so money is invested in court hire shuttles, competitions and optional training We are friendly and started SMASH partly because we’ve all been to clubs where people don’t talk to you and just want to play with their mates No transport and can’t get there?
Some committee members have room in their car, so please come along and ask a Committee Member if we have members who live/work near you.
